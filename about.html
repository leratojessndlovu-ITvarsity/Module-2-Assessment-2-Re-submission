About Pine City Mall

Description

Pine City Mall opened its doors to the public on 04 June 2025 and is Diamond City's biggest mall development and the largest project and anchor development in Diamond City

Pine City Mall is Diamond City’s largest shopping mall ever built in a single phase, and in just five months, has quickly become a leading lifestyle, entertainment and shopping destination. With over 130 000 m² of retail space, it is home to over 500 shops, many of which are flagship stores.

Voted the ‘Coolest Mall’ by Diamond City News and HDI for three consecutive years, Pine City Mall has also positioned itself as a patron of the arts. The mall is also home to the historic landmark 5.5m sculpture, “There is no time like the present”, designed by local artist, Lucia Ndlovu
